PHYSICS MODEL

240Hz fixed-timestep simulation with sub-stepping for fast objects (CCD). Impulse-based collisions with separate normal and tangential components. Friction at contact transfers linear momentum to angular velocity (spin). Spring constraints with Hooke's law plus damping. Magnus effect curves spinning trajectories through air. Heat accumulates via frictive contact and dissipates via radiation. Mass ∝ r²·density, inertia ½m·r².

RENDERING

Gradient-shaded spheres with material-aware lighting (metallic vs diffuse). Off-screen bloom pass with 2-step blur for glowing materials. Glass balls refract the scene through inverse sampling. Heat glow, ambient occlusion, squash deformation, specular highlights, drop shadows, motion blur, particle smoke trails.
